About

My transition to coding came as a result of my love of technology and thinking about the ways in which it impacts our culture. With the onset of the pandemic, I became to determnied to pursue this interest as a new career and I enrolled in General Assembly's Software Engineering Immersive program. I love the satisfaction that comes with solving complex coding problems. As a fullstack developer, I like working with Epress and Node on the back-end and watching it all come together on the front-end with Javascript and React. My approach to coding is methodical and focused on best practices because I want to get things done the right way. When I'm not learning new skills as a developer, you can find me checking out nature, taking photogrpahs or sailing on the San Francisco Bay.
